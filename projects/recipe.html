Chicken Biryani Recipe

A delicious Bangladeshi dish

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions sliced
  • 2 tomatoes chopped
  • yogurt 1 cup
  • biryani masala
  • salt, oil, ghee
  • coriander and mint leaves

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and masala for 1 hour
  2. Fry onions until golden brown
  3. Cook the marinated chicken until tender
  4. Boil rice until 70% done and drain
  5. Layer rice over chicken in a pot
  6. Add ghee, coriander and mint on top
  7. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes
  8. Serve hot with salad
